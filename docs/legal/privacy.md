# Privacy Policy — Senik

_Last updated: 2026-04-27_

This Privacy Policy describes what data Senik ("the App", "we") collects, how we use it, and your choices.

## Data we collect

**Account data (only if you sign in):**
- Email address (when you sign in via email or Google)
- A unique user ID assigned by Firebase Authentication
- Sign-in provider (email or Google)

You can use the App without signing in ("Skip for now"). In that case, no account data is collected.

**Usage data (anonymous, opt-in):**
- Card opens, swipes, scenario votes — used to compute aggregate statistics ("how many readers chose option A") and your daily streak
- These events are stored locally in the App (AsyncStorage) and, only if you are signed in, in your private user document on Firebase Firestore
- You can disable analytics at any time in Settings → Analytics

**Crash reports (opt-in):**
- Stack traces and device model are sent if the App crashes
- Used solely to fix bugs
- You can disable crash reports in Settings → Crash reports

**Notifications:**
- Daily reminder time and your preference are stored locally
- We do not send push notifications from a server unless you opt in via Settings

## What we do NOT collect

- Real name, age, gender, location (beyond device locale)
- Contacts, photos, microphone, camera
- Browsing history outside the App
- Behavior data sold to third parties

## Third-party services

- **Firebase Authentication** (Google) — to sign you in
- **Firebase Firestore** (Google) — to store your saved cards and streak across devices
- **Firebase Analytics** (Google, opt-in) — anonymous usage statistics
- **Google Play Integrity API** — verifies the App was installed from Google Play (App Check)
- **Apple App Attest** (iOS, future) — same purpose for iOS
- **RevenueCat** (when subscriptions are added) — to manage subscription state

We do not share data with advertisers or data brokers.

## Your rights

- **Access** — request a copy of your data: contact us at the email below
- **Delete** — Settings → Profile → Sign out, then Settings → Clear cache will erase local data; to delete server-side data, contact us
- **Export** — currently manual (contact us); we plan to add an export button

## Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not sign in or send us any personal information.

## Changes

We may update this Policy. The "Last updated" date will reflect the latest revision.
